About EnSolio

EnSolio was built on a simple premise: property owners and managers deserve a maintenance partner who operates like a professional — predictable, process-driven, and reliable from first contact through completion.

With a background in operations and systems, Stilian brings structured thinking to property maintenance and handyman services — coordinating punch lists, managing follow-through, and supporting preventive upkeep that keeps units functional, presentable, and easier to manage.
